CoLLearn supports out-of-school curriculum and extra curricular learning under the same roof for learners in a community based setting

Aimed at collaborative learning, we focus towards “the three C’s of learning” namely curiosity, creativity and critical thinking. The idea is to nudge the learners at an early age towards their innate areas of interest in an exploratory environment.

Targeted towards an age group of 12 to 20 years old, we offer micro courses and workshops on Robotics, Machine Learning, Design Thinking, Financial Markets, Creative Writing, Public Speaking, Photography and a lot more
